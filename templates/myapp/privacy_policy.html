Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. This policy explains what information we collect and how we are committed to protecting it.

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information You Provide to Us: This includes information submitted through our contact forms, such as your name, email address, and the content of your message. If you are a healthcare provider listing your practice, we collect professional information such as your name, specialty, qualifications, and contact details.
  • Information from Your Use of Our Site: We collect non-personally identifying information that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as your browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. We also collect information from search queries you perform on the site to improve our services and understand user needs.
  • Cookies: Like many websites, we use "cookies" to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that we transfer to your computer's hard disk for record-keeping purposes. We use cookies to understand how you interact with our site and to monitor aggregate usage and web traffic routing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website, services, and features.
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and requests and to provide customer service.
  • To understand user behavior and trends to enhance the user experience.
  • To populate our directory with accurate information about healthcare providers.
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

BdDoc24 does not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates for the purposes outlined above.

Information publicly displayed on provider profiles (such as name, specialty, and hospital affiliation) is considered public information and is accessible to all users of the site.

4. Data Security

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and data stored on our site. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights & Third-Party Links

Your Rights: You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. If you are a registered provider, you may access and update your profile information through your dashboard. For other requests or corrections, please contact us.

Third-Party Websites: Our site may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage you to read their privacy statements.

6. Changes to This Policy & Contact

BdDoc24 has the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to frequently check this page for any changes.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Contact Us.
