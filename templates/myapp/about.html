About BdDoc24

Building trust through transparency, empowering millions to make informed healthcare decisions with reliable, verified information.

Born From a Personal Struggle

BdDoc24 was born from a simple yet powerful realization: finding the right doctor in Bangladesh shouldn't be a challenge. Our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of navigating fragmented healthcare information and unreliable referrals.

What started as a personal quest has evolved into a platform built on the pillars of transparency, verification, and accessibility, connecting millions of Bangladeshis to the care they deserve.

Our Mission

To empower every individual in Bangladesh to make informed healthcare decisions by providing a comprehensive, verified, and user-friendly directory.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and indispensable digital health resource in Bangladesh, fostering a healthier nation by connecting patients to the right care.
